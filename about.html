About

OLOR DE VEIRO is a visionary beauty-retail concept founded in Ottawa by Nichole Veiro in 2013. OLOR DE VEIRO's unique, open-sell environment features an ever-increasing amount of classic and emerging brands across a broad range of product categories in fragrance for men and women

To build the most knowledgeable and professional team of product consultants in the beauty industry, OLOR DE VEIRO developed "Science of OLOR DE VEIRO." This program ensures that our team is skilled to identify scent types, have knowledge of scent physiology, application techniques, the science of creating fragrances, and most importantly, how to interact with OLOR DE VEIRO's diverse clientele.
